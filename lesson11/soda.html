Only a Few Toes Lost to Frostbite

It's that time of year. Everyone grab your dry suit and head to Lava Hot Springs for the annual Winterfest! Even if you don't want to participate, you don't want to miss a day full of fun and photo opportunities.

This year includes 13 new vendors and 3 new activities to choose from. Sign up ahead of time on Lava Hot Spring's website or get in line early. Doors open March 20th at 9 am. The Polar Bear paddle is a yearly favorite, so don't wait to sign up. You can also try one of the new activities: Three-legged Snow Shoe Race or Inter Tube Donut Roll.

This year is sure to be a blast with Ricardo's Hot Italian Chocolate to keep you warm. (First one is on the house!) Don't miss Mrs. Dibbs Toasty Cinnabuttons. (Choose your toppings!)

As always, medical professionals are on standby to create a safe atmosphere. All medical needs will be addressed promptly.
